Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with us and building your personal brand.

Why should I create a personal brand?

A strong personal brand makes opportunities come to you instead of chasing them. It positions you as an authority, builds trust with your audience, and opens doors to better clients, partnerships, and career opportunities. In today's digital world, your online presence is your reputation.

What exactly is included in your services?

Everything from idea to final delivery: content strategy, scripting, filming guidance with shot lists, professional editing, color grading, motion graphics, sound design, and multi-platform delivery. You focus on your message, we handle the rest.

Do I need to film the content myself?

Yes, but we guide you completely. We provide shot lists, technical specs, and direction so you know exactly what to capture. Most clients use just their smartphone and achieve professional results with our guidance.

How long does the process take?

5-7 days for single projects. Monthly packages are faster (2-4 days per video) since we work continuously. Rush delivery available for urgent needs.

What if I don't like the final video?

We offer unlimited revisions on all packages. We refine until you're 100% satisfied. Your success is our success.

What types of videos do you create?

All personal brand content: brand stories, educational videos, social media Reels/Shorts/TikToks, podcast clips, testimonials, behind-the-scenes, product launches, and more. If it builds your brand, we create it.

Can you help with content strategy?

Yes! Strategy is included in all packages. We help plan what to create, when to post, and how to maximize impact with a content calendar aligned to your goals.

What video formats do you deliver?

All formats needed: landscape (16:9) for YouTube, vertical (9:16) for Reels/TikTok/Shorts, square (1:1) for feeds, plus any custom sizes. All optimized per platform.

Do you provide the music and graphics?

Yes! Premium licensed music and custom motion graphics, titles, and animations are all included. No hidden fees.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, no long-term contracts. Cancel or pause anytime with 30 days notice. We want you to stay because you love the results.

What if I need more videos than my package?

Easily upgrade to a higher tier or purchase additional videos at discounted rates. We're flexible and scale with your needs.

How do we communicate during projects?

WhatsApp for quick updates and email for detailed briefs. You have direct access to your product owner anytime. Clear, constant communication.

Do you work with clients worldwide?

Yes! Fully online process via cloud storage (Google Drive, Dropbox). Time zones never an issue.
