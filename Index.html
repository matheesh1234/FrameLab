Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with us and building your personal brand.

Why should I create a personal brand?

A strong personal brand makes opportunities come to you instead of chasing them. It positions you as an authority, builds trust with your audience, and opens doors to better clients, partnerships, and career opportunities.

What exactly is included in your services?

Everything from idea to final delivery: content strategy, scripting, filming guidance with shot lists, professional editing, color grading, motion graphics, sound design, and multi-platform delivery.

Do I need to film the content myself?

Yes, but we guide you completely. We provide shot lists, technical specs, and direction so you know exactly what to capture. Most clients use just their smartphone and achieve professional results.

How long does the process take?

5-7 days for single projects. Monthly packages are faster (2-4 days per video) since we work continuously. Rush delivery available for urgent needs.

What if I don't like the final video?

We offer unlimited revisions on all packages. We refine until you're 100% satisfied. Your success is our success.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, no long-term contracts. Cancel or pause anytime with 30 days notice. We want you to stay because you love the results.
